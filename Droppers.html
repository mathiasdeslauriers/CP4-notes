What is a Dropper Post?

dropper posts have become very popular in the world of mountain biking recently. These usefull little devices allow you to push a button on your handle bars, and your seatpost will go up or down. This is useful when you are quicly changing from uphill to downhill. Having your seat out of the way allows you to move your seat out of the way. This will in turn, cause you to have a greater control during your descents.
Problem is that these devices will often set you back around 450$.
